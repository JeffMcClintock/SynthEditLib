SVF (TPT)

A 2-pole (12 dB/octave) state-variable filter built with the Topology-Preserving Transform (zero-delay feedback). The cutoff is prewarped so it stays accurate up to Nyquist, and it remains stable and in-tune at any cutoff/resonance, even under fast modulation. Low-, band- and high-pass are produced simultaneously.

Pins

  • Signal - the audio input to be filtered.
  • Pitch - the cutoff frequency (1 Volt/Octave).
  • Resonance - 0 (none) to 1 (self-oscillation).
  • Low Pass - the low-pass output (12 dB/octave).
  • Band Pass - the band-pass output.
  • High Pass - the high-pass output (12 dB/octave).
