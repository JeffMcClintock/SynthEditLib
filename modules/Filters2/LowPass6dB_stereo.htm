Low Pass (6dB) Stereo

A stereo low-pass filter with a gentle 6 dB/octave response. Two independent first-order ('one pole', leaky-integrator) low-passes sharing a single cutoff.

Pins

  • Signal L - the left audio input to be filtered.
  • Signal R - the right audio input to be filtered.
  • Pitch - the cutoff frequency for both channels (1 Volt/Octave).
  • Output L - the filtered left audio.
  • Output R - the filtered right audio.
