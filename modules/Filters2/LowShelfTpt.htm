Low Shelf (TPT)

A low-shelf equaliser built with the Topology-Preserving Transform (zero-delay feedback). Boosts or cuts everything below the corner frequency. At 0 dB it is a perfect bypass.

Pins

  • Signal - the audio input.
  • Pitch - the corner frequency (1 Volt/Octave).
  • Gain dB - boost (+) or cut (-) in decibels.
  • Q - corner sharpness (0.707 = no overshoot).
  • Output - the equalised audio.
